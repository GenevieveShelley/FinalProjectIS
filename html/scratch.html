Real Impact of Volleyball on Me

I LOVE Volleyball

Here's Why :D

  1. Hitting
    • Learning how to hit well is hard
    • You have to jump high for optimal hitting
    • You are reliant on your team to pass well
  2. Setting
    • It is amazing to get a kill off your set
    • You can do surprise back-sets #montagemoments
    • You get to touch the ball almost every play
  3. Passing
    • The feeling when you pass a dime
    • The exhileration (and fear) of getting spiked at
    • The pressure to get every ball up
